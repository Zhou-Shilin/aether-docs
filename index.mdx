Welcome to **Aether** — a locally-run AI Agent for Android. Built on the Pi framework, Aether puts a full agentic experience directly on your device: connect to any major LLM provider, automate tasks with built-in Alpine Linux tooling, and extend the agent with your own mods — all without routing your credentials through any Aether-operated service.

## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Aether from GitHub Releases">
    Download the latest APK from the [Aether Releases page](https://github.com/Zhou-Shilin/Aether/releases) and install it on your Android device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your LLM provider">
    Open Aether, navigate to **Settings → Model Providers**, and add your preferred LLM provider with an API key, OAuth, or environment credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting with your AI agent">
    Select a model in the top bar and send your first message. Enable **Agent Mode** to control apps on an isolated Android virtual display.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Aether requires **Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher** and an **arm64-v8a** device architecture. Make sure your device meets these requirements before installing.
</Note>
